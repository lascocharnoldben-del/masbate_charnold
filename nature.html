Home Page About Animasola Island Mangrovetum Halea Nature Park

Halea Nature Park is a relaxing eco-tourism destination located in Masbate, known for its peaceful environment and natural beauty. The park features man-made lagoons, gardens, and native-style cottages that create a calm and refreshing atmosphere. It is a perfect place for families and friends who want to unwind and enjoy nature.

Visitors can swim in the lagoon, take photos around the scenic areas, or simply relax under the shade of trees. The park also promotes environmental awareness by preserving its natural surroundings. With its quiet ambiance and refreshing views, Halea Nature Park is a great escape from everyday stress.
