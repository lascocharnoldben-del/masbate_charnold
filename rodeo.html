The Rodeo Masbateño Festival

Home Page About Animasola Island Mangrovetum Halea Nature Park

The Rodeo Masbateño Festival is an annual, week-long celebration in Masbate City (the "Rodeo Capital of the Philippines") showcasing cowboy culture, featuring national rodeo finals, cattle drives, and livestock shows. Held between April and June, it promotes the province's cattle industry through exciting competitions like bull riding and lassoing, drawing many tourists
