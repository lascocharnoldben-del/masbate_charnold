Palani White Beachis one of the most stunning beaches in Masbate, known for its long stretch of fine white sand. The beach is lined with coconut trees and surrounded by clear blue waters, creating a peaceful and tropical atmosphere. It is an ideal place for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing by the shore.
