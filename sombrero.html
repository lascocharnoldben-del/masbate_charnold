Sombrero Island in San Pascual, Masbate, is a popular destination in the Burias Group of Islands known for its distinct hat-shaped rock formation, powdery white sand beaches, and crystal-clear waters. Ideal for camping and snorkeling, it is a key stopover for beach hopping, with limited amenities like rustic cottages, basic restrooms, and small, local stores.
