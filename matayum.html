Matayum Lagoon

Home Page About Animasola Island Mangrovetum Halea Nature Park

Matayum Lagoon is a beautiful hidden gem in Masbate, famous for its crystal-clear blue water surrounded by natural rock formations. The lagoon has calm and refreshing water, making it perfect for swimming and relaxing. Its peaceful and quiet environment makes it ideal for visitors who want to escape crowded places.

The area is also great for photography because of its vibrant colors and scenic views. Visitors can explore the surroundings while enjoying the cool and relaxing atmosphere. With its natural beauty and tranquility, Matayum Lagoon is a must-visit destination in Masbate.
