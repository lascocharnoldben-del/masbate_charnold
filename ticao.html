Ticao Island is one of the three main islands of Masbate and is known for its rich marine biodiversity and scenic coastal landscapes. It is home to the famous Manta Bowl Shoal, where divers can experience swimming with manta rays and other marine species. The island features beautiful beaches, cliffs, and clear waters that attract nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Aside from diving, visitors can enjoy island hopping, snorkeling, and exploring nearby hidden spots. The peaceful environment and natural beauty make it a great destination for relaxation and outdoor activities. With its unique marine life and breathtaking views, Ticao Island is a must-visit in Masbate.
