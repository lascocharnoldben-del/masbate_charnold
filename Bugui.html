Bugui Point Lighthouse is a scenic landmark located in Masbate, offering breathtaking views of the sea and surrounding coastline. The lighthouse stands on a high point, making it a perfect spot for sightseeing and photography. Visitors often come here to enjoy the cool breeze and peaceful atmosphere. It is especially beautiful during sunrise and sunset, when the sky and ocean create stunning colors. The area around the lighthouse is quiet and less crowded, making it ideal for relaxation. With its panoramic views and calm surroundings, Bugui Point Lighthouse is a hidden gem worth visiting in Masbate.
