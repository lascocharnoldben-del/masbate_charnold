Borobangcaso Rock Formation

Home Page About Animasola Island Mangrovetum Halea Nature Park

Borobangcaso Rock Formation is a unique coastal attraction in Masbate known for its rugged and naturally shaped rocks. The area features large rock formations along the shoreline, where waves continuously crash and create a dramatic view. It is a great spot for photography and enjoying the raw beauty of nature.
