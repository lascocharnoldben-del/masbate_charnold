Home Page About Animasola Island Mangrovetum Halea Nature Park

Mangrovetum in Masbate City is a peaceful mangrove sanctuary that showcases the importance of protecting coastal ecosystems. It features a variety of mangrove trees that help prevent erosion and serve as a habitat for marine life. Visitors can walk along the wooden pathways and enjoy the calm, nature-filled surroundings..

The area is also use d for educational purposes, teaching people about environmental conservation and sustainability. It is a great place for relaxation, photography, and learning about nature. With its quiet atmosphere and natural beauty, Mangrovetum is a perfect escape from the busy city.
