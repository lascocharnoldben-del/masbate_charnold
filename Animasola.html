Animasola Island

Home Page About Animasola Island Mangrovetum Halea Nature Park

Animasola Island in San Pascual, Masbate, is a stunning, off-the-beaten-path destination known for its dramatic, layered sandstone rock formations and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Often described as a "Mermaid's Lair" or the "Kapurpurawan of Masbate," this serene, "lonely soul" island features surreal, wavy rock structures best explored via boat from San Pascual or Quezon Province.
